About Fiona Creations

The Fiona Creations Experience

Our mission is to make high-end, bespoke fashion accessible and sustainable. Fiona Creations is a Pretoria-based fashion brand dedicated to redefining elegance for life's most special moments. We specialize in luxury dress rentals, custom-made gowns, and personal styling services designed exclusively for women attending weddings and formal events.

Our clientele includes brides, bridesmaids, and female wedding guests who value both style and convenience. To make the experience effortless, we also offer a premium out-of-office styling service, bringing fittings and consultations directly to you — whether at home, your office, or your event venue.

Beyond style, our mission is rooted in sustainability. By promoting re-wearable fashion and eco-conscious practices, we aim to reduce waste in the fashion industry while making luxury more accessible. Every piece in our collection is thoughtfully crafted and maintained with care, ensuring that each gown carries a story of elegance and responsibility.
